About me

Vinesh Redkar is a seasoned security professional with over 10 years of experience across diverse industries, including finance, insurance, telecommunications, and government sectors.

Currently serving as a Senior Security Architect at Emirates NBD, he is instrumental in ensuring security integration throughout the agile development lifecycle and implementing secure designs during project execution. In his role, Vinesh manages and oversees external vendor resources and is responsible for security reviews, ensuring adherence to the highest quality standards during security testing. His extensive hands-on expertise spans security testing (web, mobile, and infrastructure), security architecture, vulnerability assessment and penetration testing, risk analysis, and resource management. Additionally, Vinesh is the author of the CIS Security Benchmark for MongoDB Database and actively contributes as a Private Bug Bounty Hunter, further demonstrating his commitment to advancing the field of cybersecurity
